Sounds Of New York City (SONYC)

Website SONYC    Sep, 2017 - Present

Working on sensor maintenance and microphone fault detection for the largest distributed noise monitor network in the world.

SONYC is one of the main reasons that I decided to come to NYC and join the Urban Informatics program at CUSP. It was the bridge between my interests and training in acoustics and my enthusiasm for machine learning. Since I've been working on the team, I've done work in:

  • Microphone fault detection, looking to identify static-like microphone failure using deep learning and characteristics from their audio stream.
  • Sensor deployment and maintanance, ensuring that the network stays up and running.
  • Upgrading and creating dashboards for monitoring the network's health.
  • Adding an alerting system that will notify the maintanance team of any anomalous behavior.
  • Working to retrofit one our sensors to use GPS tracking so that the sensor can be attached to a mobile source.
