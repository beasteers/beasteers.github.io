Website University of Hartford    Sep, 2013 - May, 2017

Projects

Quantifying Tranquility in Urban Environments - 2017

Identified tranquil locations in cities by investigating the perceptual features of tranquility across visual and auditory modalities in downtown Hartford, CT and Central Park, NY.

Instrument Directivity Approximation For Use In Concert Hall Measurements - 2016

Used an icosahedron omnidirectional loudspeaker to reproduce instrument directivities to help give a more realistic reproduction of an orchestra when testing concert halls.

Study Of Computational Auditory Models - 2016

Study of signal processing and audio feature extraction to be used to train probabilistic auditory models for real-time audio analysis.

Community-driven Acoustics Phone App - 2016

Developed a web API to handle data acquisition, processing, and distribution for geo- encoded sound level data collected by app users.

Acoustical Consulting Project - 2015

Took acoustic measurements of an office space with reported speech intelligibility issues and provided solutions as a comprehensive engineering report.
